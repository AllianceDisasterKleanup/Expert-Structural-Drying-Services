Specialized Equipment That We Use

The Alliance Disaster Kleenup teams take into account the structure itself, building materials, seasons, and indoor conditions when formulating a plan to begin the drying process. Once the plan has been put into motion you can rest assured that all moisture will be removed from the structure and the rebuilding can begin. In order to do our best, we utilize highly advanced equipment including:

Infrared Camera

Assists in finding and tracking hidden moisture.

Refrigerant Dehumidifiers

These are powerful machines that can assist in speeding up the process of drying any structure.

Dessicant Dehumidifiers

These are mainly used during the drying process of large buildings and are capable of efficiently drying the largest of structures.

Injectidry System

Assists in creating airflow in difficult to reach and/or complicated areas.
